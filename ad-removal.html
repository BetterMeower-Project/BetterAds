BetterAds Removal

We understand that you may not like a certain ad you see, which is why we have an easy way to remove that ad.

Understanding the ad remover

Ad removal is not permanent. You may continue to see this ad on different sites or after a refresh, this feature simply allows you to temporarily hide an ad from a web page.

Using the ad remover

To use the remover, simply click the X button in the top right corner of the ad.

Reporting an ad

All ads are reviewed by human moderators before being let through, however we do sometimes accidentally let an innapropriate ad through, If you so happen to come across one of these, please send an email to us at adservice@bettermeower.app. Please make sure that you include a screenshot.
